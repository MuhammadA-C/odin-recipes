Tacos Recipe

Description

Here we will teach you how to make a delicious taco!

Ingredients

  • Shredded cheese
  • Ground Turkey or Beef
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Paparika
  • Taco Shells
  • Sour Cream
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Lettuce
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Add ground turkey or beef to a pan on the stove with seasonings and mix the seasonings in
  2. Add meat to taco shell
  3. Layer shredded cheese ontop
  4. Layer sour cream on top
