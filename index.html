Burger

Description

The recipe to make this burger is fairly simple. All you'll need is sliched american cheese, ground turkey or beef, your choice of condiments, and salt, black pepper, onion powder, paparika, & garlic powder.

Ingredients

  • Ground Turkey or Beef
  • American Cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paparika
  • Burger Buns
  • Your choice of BBQ sauce, Ketchup, Mustard, etc...

Steps

  1. Add ground turkey or beef to bowl
  2. Add seasoning to meat
  3. Mix seasoning into meat
  4. Make meat into round balls
  5. Turn on stove and place pan over top
  6. Add oil to pan
  7. Wait until oil heats up to add meat
